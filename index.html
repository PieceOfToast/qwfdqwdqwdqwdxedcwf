RESEARCH INTERESTS

My main research goal is to understand the evolution and composition of planetary interiors through high-pressure simulation experiments using piston-cylinder apparatus, multi-anvil devices, diamond-anvil cell, and dynamic compression methods. I also pursue new materials synthesis at high pressure and temperature.

  • Earth and planet evolution

  • Planetary interior processes

  • Compositions of the Earth's mantle and core

  • Physical properties of materials at high P and T

  • Materials synthesis at high pressure

  • Development of high-pressure techniques

New mineral name: Feiite

IMA Commission approved a new mineral, Feiite, named after mineralogist Yingwei Fei. Feiite, a titanium iron oxide with formula Fe2(FeTi)O5 and orthorhombic structure, was discovered in a shocked meteorite from Mars.
